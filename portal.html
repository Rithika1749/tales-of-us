The Portal

The portal is a dimensional gateway that connects two worlds: Earthland and Magica. It was created as the result of a powerful spell gone wrong, tearing a permanent opening between realities.

Over time, the instability of the portal allows creatures such as ghouls, spirits, ghosts, vampires, and werewolves to cross into Earthland. Their presence disrupts the natural balance of both worlds.

In response, mages and knights from Magica enter Earthland through the same gateway to either vanquish these creatures or return them to their rightful realm.

Visually, the portal appears as an ancient wooden door suspended in space. Its surface is engraved with delicate forget-me-not flowers, faintly glowing when the boundary between worlds weakens.
