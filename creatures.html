Creatures from the Other World

Vampires
Strengths: Superhuman speed, strength, regeneration
Weaknesses: Sunlight, silver, holy magic
Notes: Intelligent and highly strategic
Werewolves
Strengths: Immense physical power, rapid healing
Weaknesses: Silver weapons, loss of control under moonlight
Notes: Fiercely loyal but volatile
Ghouls
Strengths: Pack coordination, pain resistance
Weaknesses: Fire, light-based magic
Notes: Mindless and aggressive
Spirits
Strengths: Intangibility, possession abilities
Weaknesses: Binding spells, enchanted seals
Notes: Emotion-driven entities
Ghosts
Strengths: Invisibility, fear manipulation
Weaknesses: Sacred wards, memory-based magic
Notes: Anchored to unfinished emotions
