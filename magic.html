Magic Rules

Magic can only be used by individuals who possess a natural affinity for magic. This affinity is linked to emotional sensitivity and resonance with specific elements.

Attempting to use magic without affinity results in severe physical and mental consequences.

Elemental Magic
Function: Manipulation of natural elements
Strengths: High destructive potential
Weaknesses: Emotion-dependent control
Mana Cost: High
Spatial & Time Magic
Function: Alters space and temporal flow
Strengths: Reality-level influence
Weaknesses: Extremely unstable
Mana Cost: Very High
Healing Magic
Function: Restoration and recovery
Strengths: Preserves life
Weaknesses: Cannot resurrect the dead
Mana Cost: Moderate
Binding Magic
Function: Sealing and containment
Strengths: Control over entities
Weaknesses: Requires preparation
Mana Cost: Low to Moderate

Mana is finite and regenerates slowly. Overuse may cause exhaustion, loss of consciousness, or permanent damage to magical ability.
